TuneIn AIR Integration

Get more attention on the TuneIn directory!

This free integration allows you to send your currently playing song information to the TuneIn directory. Be more noticed on TuneIn and tell your listeners what you're currently playing!

If you're not already listed on TuneIn, this won't work - you will need to ensure you have a valid station listing first!
